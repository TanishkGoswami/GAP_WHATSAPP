# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 2026

Welcome to **WhatsApp Automation** (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our WhatsApp automation services, including our integrations with the Meta (Facebook) WhatsApp Cloud API.

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
When you register for an account or use our services, we may collect personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number (including your WhatsApp Business number)
- Billing and payment information

### B. Information Collected via Meta Integration (WhatsApp Cloud API)
Our App integrates with the WhatsApp Business Platform via the Meta Cloud API. To provide our services, we access and process certain data with your consent:
- **WhatsApp Messages:** We process incoming and outgoing messages (text, media, attachments) to provide automation, auto-replies, and live chat features.
- **Contact Information:** Phone numbers and profile names of the customers interacting with your WhatsApp Business Account.
- **Account Metadata:** Information regarding your WhatsApp Business Account status, templates, and analytics.

### C. Usage Data
We automatically collect information about your interaction with our App, such as IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, referring URLs, and pages viewed.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for various purposes, including to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the App's core functionality (e.g., routing WhatsApp messages, triggering automation flows).
- Authenticate your Meta/Facebook account and link your WhatsApp Business Account.
- Improve, personalize, and expand our services.
- Process transactions and send related information (e.g., invoices).
- Send administrative emails, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
- Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve user experience.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information only in the following situations:
- **With Meta (Facebook):** As a required part of using the WhatsApp Cloud API, data such as messages and account status are transmitted between our App and Meta's servers according to Meta's own Data Processing Terms.
- **Service Providers:** We may share data with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting providers like Supabase, payment processors).
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency).

## 4. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal information and message data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, or as required by law. 

**Account Deletion:** You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data. If you choose to delete your account or unlink your Meta account, your associated WhatsApp tokens, automated flows, and locally stored chat data will be permanently deleted from our active databases.

## 5. Security of Your Data

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures (such as encryption and secure server hosting) to help protect your personal information. However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children under 13. 

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
